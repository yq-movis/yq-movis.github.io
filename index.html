Master’s Research Project (MRP)

My MRP is a collaborative project with Nichole Zhou called Empowering Youth: Career Exploration through Narrative Gameplay. It is a 2D interactive storytelling game with word puzzles and multiple endings determined by player decisions. We aim to create a realistic peek into student experiences within STEM career pathways and inspire prospective student players in their own journeys.
